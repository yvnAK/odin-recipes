Easy Churro Cronuts Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 package refrigerated biscuit dough
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup chocolate sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cut holes in the center of each biscuit to form rings.
  3. Fry until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Mix sugar and cinnamon in a bowl and coat the fried cronuts.
  5. Serve with chocolate sauce for dipping.
