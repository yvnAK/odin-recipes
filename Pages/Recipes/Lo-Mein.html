Chilled Lo Mein Noodles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces lo mein noodles
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon Szechuan peppercorns
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar

Instructions

  1. Cook lo mein noodles according to package instructions; drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a skillet, heat sesame oil and toast Szechuan peppercorns.
  3. Add bell pepper, carrot, and green onions; stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Toss noodles with vegetables, soy sauce, and rice vinegar.
  5. Chill before serving.
