Caramel Apple Cheesecake Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 (8-ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup caramel sauce, plus more for garnish
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 8 honeycrisp apples, cored and chopped into 1/4-inch pieces
  • 1 (9-inch) graham cracker pie crust, broken into pieces

Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat together cream cheese, caramel sauce, powdered sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon in a large bowl using an electric mixer until creamy.
  3. Add heavy cream and continue beating on medium-high for about 2 minutes or until thickened and fluffy.
  4. Gently fold in apples until combined.
  5. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours or until thickened.
  6. Just before serving, gently fold in graham cracker crust pieces. Drizzle with additional caramel sauce, if desired.
