No-Bake Cannoli Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 (9-inch) graham cracker crust
  • 1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup chopped pistachios

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix ricotta, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  2. Fold in chocolate chips and pistachios.
  3. Spread the mixture into the graham cracker crust.
  4. Chill for at least 4 hours before serving.
