Lobster Vanilla Bean Bisque Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 lobsters, cooked and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup seafood stock
  • 1 vanilla bean, split and scraped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat olive oil and saut√© onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add lobster, seafood stock, and vanilla bean; simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in heavy cream and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Blend until smooth and serve hot.
