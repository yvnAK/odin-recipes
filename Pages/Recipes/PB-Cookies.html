Cornflake Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups corn flakes

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg until smooth.
  3. Fold in corn flakes until well combined.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
