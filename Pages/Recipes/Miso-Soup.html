Miso Noodle Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup miso paste
  • 1 cup cooked noodles
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 4 cups hot water

Instructions

  1. In a jar, layer miso paste, noodles, mushrooms, and green onions.
  2. Add soy sauce on top.
  3. When ready to eat, pour hot water into the jar and stir until miso is dissolved.
  4. Let sit for a few minutes before enjoying.
