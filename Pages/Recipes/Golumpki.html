Beef and Cabbage (Lazy Golumpki) Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 head cabbage, chopped
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a skillet, brown ground beef and onion.
  2. Add cabbage and cook until wilted.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce and cooked rice; season with salt and pepper.
