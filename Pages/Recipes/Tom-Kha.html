Turkey Tom Kha Gai Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound turkey breast, sliced thin
  • 1 can (14 ounces) coconut milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, cut into 2-inch pieces and smashed
  • 3-4 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1-2 Thai bird chilies, smashed (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pot, combine chicken broth, coconut milk, lemongrass, lime leaves, and chilies; bring to a simmer.
  2. Add turkey slices and mushrooms; cook until turkey is cooked through.
  3. Stir in fish sauce and lime juice; adjust seasoning to taste.
  4. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.
